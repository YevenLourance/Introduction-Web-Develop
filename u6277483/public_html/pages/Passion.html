Drawing types

  1. Painting

    I drew Chinese painting years ago. Now I normally paint some watercolour in spare time this year. Thus, I am just a beginner of watercolour painting. Here are a collection of my watercolour paintings. I am still on my way in practicing mixing colours and watercolour is really a kind of magic in free play of water.


  2. Sketching

    Simple pencil sketching help me to understand the 3D structure of objects and how we visualise objects in 2D plane. I will practice some portait sketching from time to time to practice my skills to master objects structure and cognition.


  3. Colour Pencil

    I learn colour pencil sketching by myself. It takes a lot of time to finish one colour pencil drawing, but I still think it is the easiest way to express colours in an artistic way. It is similar to normal sketching except we have colours to represent what we observe.
